Welcome to our website

We are members of the Multimedia Mission at the King's Gate United church, a community of believers dedicated to spreading the word of God and supporting one another on our spiritual journeys.

Take a look around to learn more about us and our mission.

What We Offer

At Our Church, we offer a variety of services and activities to help you connect with God and our community. Here are some of the things you can expect from us:

  • Online Bible study every Tuesday at 6:30 pm led by our Houses
  • Sunday worship services every Sunday at 9:00 am in the Sanctuary and online
  • Youth and children's programs held in our Church Hall
  • Social events and community outreach

Explore our website to learn more about our programs and services, and how we can help you grow in your faith.
