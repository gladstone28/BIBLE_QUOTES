About Us

We are members of the Multimedia Mission at the King's Gate United Church, a group of people working with others, to develop this website as well as a Bible app for sharing Bible quotes with the world!

In developing the app our goal is to make it easy for people to discover and share Bible quotes that inspire and uplift them. We believe that the Bible is a powerful source of wisdom and guidance, and we want to make it more accessible to everyone.
